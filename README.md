## üîê 5. THE HANDOVER PROTOCOL (Chain of Custody)

This feature is critical for Consultants selling this system. It allows you to build the product, save it, wipe your personal traces, and hand it to the client securely.

### Phase 1: Export Identity (The "Soul")
Before you wipe the system, you must save the configuration.
1.  Go to **Admin Dashboard > System**.
2.  Click **"Export Identity Key"**.
3.  This downloads a `.json` file containing:
    *   The Theme (Colors, Fonts).
    *   The License Tier (Enterprise, Estate, etc.).
    *   The VPN Configuration.
    *   **The Active Logo (Base64).**
    *   *Note: It does NOT save your private Payment Keys for security.*

### Phase 2: System Detach (The "Wipe")
1.  In **Admin Dashboard > System**, scroll to the Red Zone.
2.  Check the waivers (confirming you have removed keys and saved your backup).
3.  Click **"Execute Detach Protocol"**.
4.  **Result:** The screen goes black/dark. The system is now an empty shell (Bootloader Mode). It has forgotten you.

### Phase 3: The Rebirth (Client Activation)
This is what your client does when they receive the system.
1.  Send the URL to your client.
2.  They will see the **"System Boot"** screen.
3.  **Option A (Identity Key):** They drag-and-drop the `.json` Identity Key you sent them. The system "Boots up" with your design, but empty data.
4.  **Option B (Quick Start):** If demoing, click **"Manual Override"** to force a boot with factory settings.

## üåê 6. THE HUB & SPOKE MODEL (Architecture)

This is the advanced operational model for running multiple stores or clients.

### The Question: "Where does AIin5 fit?"
**AIin5 lives IN the Store, but it reports TO the Hub.**

### The Workflow: "Selling the Store"

1.  **The Hook (The Store):**
    *   A visitor comes to a Client Store (e.g., "Holiday Gift Shop").
    *   The **AIin5 Widget** (floating orb) asks: *"Do you like this store? I can build one for you in 5 minutes."*
    *   The visitor says "Yes". The AI captures their email as a Lead.

2.  **The Build (The Hub):**
    *   You (The Consultant) receive the lead in your **BOSS Hub**.
    *   You open your local machine, configure a new store template, and export the `.json`.

3.  **The Handover (The Session):**
    *   You meet the client on Zoom.
    *   You send them a link to a **Fresh VdM Instance**.
    *   You send them the `.json` file you generated.
    *   They upload it. The store "boots up" instantly.

### Why this is smart:
*   **Security:** The client never touches your Hub. They only get the specific file you exported.
*   **Speed:** You don't build from scratch. You pull from your inventory.
*   **Sales:** The store sells itself via the embedded AI widget.
